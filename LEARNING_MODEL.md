# Learning model

Tutor's working model of the student's current understanding.
Fast-changing — update at the triggers defined in `CLAUDE.md`.
Revise in place; no changelog.

## Skill matrix

State per concept: `unknown` | `introduced` | `can-recognize` |
`can-explain` | `can-direct` | `can-design-with`. Advance only on
observed evidence (explain-back, exercise critique, unprompted correct
use). Group entries by sprint as topics are covered.

_(empty — populated as topics are covered)_

## Misconceptions

Things the student got wrong, with the correct framing. Used to
pre-empt the same wrong turn later. One entry per misconception;
revise in place when the misconception is cleared.

_(empty)_

## Resonant analogies

Explanations that landed. Reuse and extend; do not reinvent. Note
the concept and the analogy that worked.

_(empty)_

## Open threads

Promises and parked topics — things to revisit later. Append
immediately when made; do not rely on memory. Remove when resolved.

_(empty)_

## Tentative signals

One-off observations not yet promoted to `BACKGROUND.md`. Format:
"`<observation>` — observed N times." Promote to `BACKGROUND.md`
only after recurrence ≥ 2 more or explicit confirmation by the
student.
